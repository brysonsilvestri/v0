Tips for Best Results

Use Good Lighting

Take photos in well-lit environments or near windows. Natural light works best. Avoid harsh shadows and dark backgrounds for optimal AI processing.

Center Your Product

Position the product in the center of the frame. Make sure it takes up at least 60-70% of the image for the best isolation and background removal.

High Resolution Images

Upload the highest quality photos possible. Modern smartphone cameras work great. Avoid heavily compressed or pixelated images.

Minimize Clutter

Remove unnecessary objects from the frame. The cleaner the original photo, the better the AI can isolate your product and create professional results.

Try Different Angles

Experiment with multiple angles and perspectives. Front-facing shots work best for most products, while side angles can showcase features for items like shoes or electronics.
